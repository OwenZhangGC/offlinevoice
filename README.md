## Why OfflineVoice

- **Fastest, because it's local** — no network round-trip. Speech is recognized
  on-device and the text appears almost the instant you stop talking.
- **100% on-device** — recognition runs on your Mac. Audio and text never leave
  the device. No account, no subscription, no cloud.
- **Works anywhere** — pastes into the focused text field across native Mac apps.
- **Hold-to-talk** — hold **Right Option** (configurable), speak, release to paste.
- **Two recognition modes you can pick:**
  - **Speed (default)** — Apple's native on-device recognition. Near-instant,
    nothing to download.
  - **Accuracy** — Whisper (large-v3 turbo), stronger for English and technical
    speech. Downloads once on first use, then runs offline.
- **Chinese + English** — built for Chinese, English, and mixed-language dictation.

## System requirements

**Required**

- **Apple Silicon Mac (M1 or newer).** Intel Macs are not supported.
- **macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later.**

**Speed mode (default)**

- Uses macOS's built-in on-device speech recognition — no model download.
- Make sure Dictation is enabled once in **System Settings ▸ Keyboard** so macOS
  has the on-device language model installed.

**Accuracy mode (optional)**

- Downloads the Whisper model once on first use (~1.5 GB) and caches it for
  offline use afterward. Needs internet for that first download only.
- **~2 GB free disk** and **16 GB unified memory** recommended for a comfortable
  experience in this mode.

## Install

1. Open the DMG and drag **OfflineVoice** to Applications, then launch it.
2. Grant **Microphone**, **Speech Recognition**, and **Accessibility** when
   prompted (Accessibility is what lets OfflineVoice paste into other apps).
3. Hold **Right Option**, speak, release.

## FAQ

**Does it run on Intel Macs?**
No. OfflineVoice requires an Apple Silicon Mac (M1 or newer).

**Does it need the internet?**
Speed mode (the default) needs no download and works offline out of the box.
Accuracy mode downloads the Whisper model once on first use, then runs fully
offline.

**Speed vs Accuracy — which should I use?**
Start with Speed: it's near-instant and great for everyday Chinese and English.
Switch to Accuracy in **Privacy & Local AI** when you want extra precision for
English or technical speech and don't mind the one-time model download.

**Is the download safe to open?**
Yes — it's signed with an Apple Developer ID and notarized by Apple, so it opens
with no Gatekeeper warning. Only download from the official channels above.

**Which languages does it handle?**
Chinese, English, and mixed Chinese-English dictation.

## Privacy

OfflineVoice does not upload your audio, does not sync transcripts, and does not
train on your data. Recognition runs on-device; any model files are cached
locally. Full [privacy policy](https://www.offlinevoice.ai/#privacy-policy).
